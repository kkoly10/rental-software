**Date**: June 2026
**Question answered**: How does Korent reach its first 50 paying customers and what's the positioning vs Goodshuffle Pro?

---

## Executive summary

The fastest GTM motion is **SEO-led PLG anchored on a Goodshuffle Pro head-to-head comparison page** + **Facebook group community engagement** in target US tier-2/3 metros. With Korent already shipping the features Goodshuffle is missing (SMS, portal, white-label, AI, bilingual UI) at $99/mo vs Goodshuffle's $257/mo realistic cost, the wedge is clear and the content can write itself. For Mexico (Track B, parallel), a **Workana freelance SDR at $1,000/mo** + **Expo Tu Boda booths** + **Facebook groups for renta de mobiliario** is the cheapest way to test the market while the Mexican entity setup runs in the background. The 30-article SEO calendar front-loads 6 "money pages" (comparisons + pricing calculator + contract template) in weeks 1-3 because these capture buyers already evaluating tools.

## 3. US tier-2/3 GTM tactics

### Top 3 acquisition channels

1. **Facebook Groups** — "Bounce House Business Owners," "Party Rental Business Owners," "Event Rental Professionals." Post helpful content (Excel-to-Korent migration walkthrough, pricing calculator, free template), not pitches. Aim: 5 inbound demos/week by week 6.

2. **Cold outreach via Apollo + Loom-demo videos** — scrape Google Maps for "bounce house rental Tampa" etc., send 50 personalized DMs/day with 90-second product walkthrough. Aim: 1% response → 3 demos/week.

3. **SEO long-tail content** — 30-article calendar above. Targets specific worries, ranks because nobody writes this for Goodshuffle's price-point audience.

- Solo or 2-employee party rental operators

## 4. Mexico GTM tactics (Track B, parallel)

### Hiring path (founder doesn't speak Spanish)

- **Workana freelance SDR**: $500-$1,500 USD/mo for part-time bilingual outbound. 212+ MX SDRs listed on Workana.
- **Full-time bilingual SDR**: $3,600-$6,500 USD/mo (local CDMX hire ~$1,750 USD base)

### Top channels

1. **SDR-led outbound on Facebook Groups** — "RENTA Y VENTA DE MOBILIARIO PARA FIESTAS" + Marketplace listings. SDR DMs operators directly, replies helpfully in Spanish.

2. **Wedding planner Instagram DMs** — SDR pitches operators via referrals from planners (planners get 20% kickback for first 3 months).

3. **Expo Tu Boda Monterrey Feb 21-22, 2026** — share a booth with a friendly non-competing vendor (e.g., invoice/accounting software) for $1.5-2k. Walk away with 50 qualified leads.

### Target ICP

- 1-4 person family party rental businesses
- Cities (in order of priority): **León/Bajío, Mérida** (less saturated, SDR more impactful per call), then CDMX/Monterrey
- Revenue ~$80,000 MXN/mo ($4-5k USD)

## 6. Risk register

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Goodshuffle Pro launches a cheaper tier | Ship WhatsApp + deepen SMS automation faster than they can react. Move-cost asymmetry favors smaller team. |
| Mexican entity setup blows past 4 months | Don't take MX customers until accountant confirms IVA filed. Use entity-pending time to ship US growth. |
| US tier-2 CAC stays >$250 | Pivot to pure inbound (SEO + community). Pause paid. Use content lead-magnets to qualify before sales touch. |
| Stripe MX denies merchant application | Fall back to Conekta (~18 dev-days). Confirm in writing during week 1 of entity setup. |
| Booqable launches party-rental-specific features (driver app, SMS) | Increase focus on bilingual + WhatsApp wedge. Their EU-first roadmap suggests this is unlikely Q3 2026. |
| Eventrix raises a round and out-ships Korent in Mexico | Differentiate on CFDI quality + WhatsApp depth + crew mobile workspace. Mexican entity gates this competition window. |
| SDR doesn't convert | Swap quickly; Workana has supply. Don't sign annual contracts. |
| WhatsApp Business API rejection | Use Twilio's WhatsApp BSP which has higher approval rates. Spike before committing. |
